Hiperos 3PM

  • Summary

    A four-month long project to dockerize third party framework.

  • Methods

    DevOps, docker, vagrant, puppet, kubernetes, prototyping

  • Client

    Opus

Hiperos 3PM is a third party manangement service which helps companies mitagate risks associated with 3rd and 4th party vendors. The service was in transition when I stepped into this team. They were moving from an on-premises setup to a cloud architecture.

My contributions to the project included working on bleeding edge virtualization techniques for Windows Containers. I made numerous contributions to open source projects such as Vagrant and Puppet to support my team.

In addition to giving back to the community, I also prototyped many different solutions for moving 3PM into the cloud such as using Kubernetes, Docker Swarm, and AWS Auto Scale. To keep their production, CI, and local builds cohesive, I worked on having vagrant make use of the docker containers.

While this was a short project, I accomplished my goal of taking charge of their AWS account and documenting important resources and finding cost savings by turning of unneeded resources. Also, the numerous cloud architectures I prototyped helped the business leadership make key decisions about the future direction of thier cloud.
