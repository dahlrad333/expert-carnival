Wealth Insight

  • Summary

    Wealth Insight is a service offered to individuals meeting a certain level of wealth. This service allows individuals to take transparent control of their investments.

  • Methods

    Angular, JavaScript, HTML5/CSS3, wireframing

  • Client

    PNC

PROBLEM

As an aging bank, PNC houses data on many different mainframes [yes, those still exist]. As each person is unique, in the same way accounts in these mainframes are also very unique resulting in odd combinations of data to display when the customer logs in. This is fine for the average Joe, but for their premier customers PNC would like to offer a more unified and polished look and feel.

DATA AT OUR FINGERTIPS

First, we started by defining which users would be offered this service. This strata was defined by a combination of data sources that were available to the website. Once we identified this strata, we started modifying a few pages. During my time there, I contributed to about three full pages of changes.
