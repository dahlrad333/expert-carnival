My PHN

  • Summary

    Cross-platform native mobile app for Primary Health Network.

  • Methods

    Android, iOS, mobile development, wireframing, full-stack development

  • Client

    The Primary Health Network in conjunction with Pleso Capital.

Primary Health Network Marketing Department approached me to build a mobile app for them to promote their brand and increase awareness. We started the project by agreeing on a feature set and a timeline to achieve milestones.

I was able to work remotely with their team on getting wireframes and other assets for the app throughout development, though occasionally I would drop by their office to work on complex aspects such as app store submission or when I wanted to get certain features signed off on.

The people of Primary Health Network are a fun bunch to work with, and I am sure they will consider using me for future projects.
