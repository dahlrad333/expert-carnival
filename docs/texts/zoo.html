FocusFeed

  • Summary

    A SaaS platform for aggregating online services in one dashboard. My team helped connect outsourced solutions into a coherent service.

  • Methods

    Full stack development, DevOps, startup, wireframing, market analysis, SaaS, AWS, cloud

  • Client

    FocusFeed [now defunct]

THE PROBLEM

With the myriad of cloud based tools growing each day, trying to more than a few at the same time could be distracting. This can result in lost productivity from the hours of searching and switching tabs that can result.

THE GOAL

When I was brought into this project, there were two other external vendors which had created the front and back ends. It was my job to reconcile these solutions, guide the team, and create new plugins to connect additional serivices to our platform.

THE PROCESS

With less than three months of funding, our team worked quickly to acomplish our goals. Along our path, I conducted code reviews, provided reports to C-level people, and created a CI/CD pipeline.

Unfortunately, with no customers, and a short runway, the company ran out of money, and was unable to secure A round funding to keep the project going. It is a cool idea, and I just think circumstances prevented the company from really taking off.
